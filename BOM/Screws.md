# Screws & Inserts

Here's a list of all screws and inserts needed to build your TNY-360.

A complete kit of M2.5 Screws and Inserts is also linked below for easier sourcing, instead of buying each screws/inserts independently.

### Assembly guide

To learn how to fully assemble you TNY-360, check out the [TNY-360 Documentation](http://localhost:3000/docs/tny-360/practical-guide/sourcing/). A more user-friendly list of all the screws and inserts needed is also available here.
